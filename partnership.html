Cornerstone Community Development Corporation is a 501(c)(3) non-profit faith-based organization. Our programs are offered to the community at little or no cost to the participants. Therefore, we rely on the support and generosity of those individuals and organizations committed to community wellness. Any funding we receive goes directly to support our programs. Community partners help provide necessary resources to produce sound effective programs.
