We Need You

Please Make a Donation

Cornerstone Community Development Corporation is a 501(c)(3) non-profit faith-based organization. Our programs are offered to community members at little or no cost. Therefore, we rely on the support and generosity of those individuals and organizations, like yourself, who are committed to community wellness. Any funding we receive goes directly to support our programs, i.e. they are not allocated to indirect or administrative costs. Your donations help provide the necessary resources to produce sound effective programs.
