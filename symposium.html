"Establishing and Maintaining Growth in the Face of Challenge"

Entrepreneurial guru, Erike Mayo, a Certified Small Business Consultant and Instructor with NYC Dept of Small Business Services, will lead the panel of business leaders in discussing how to thrive in the current economic and political environment. The panel will reflect entrepreneurial, financial, human resource, and operational expertise.


"A Right to Wellness - Healthcare Alternatives for the Small Business & Micro-enterprise"

For small business owners and the self-employed, this is a unique opportunity to participate in an informative discussion with government and industry experts, as they address healthcare insurance coverage for the self-employed & small busi-nesses. The discussion will focus on vehicles that are available to help lower the cost of health insurance premiums and the law in New Jersey as it pertains to health coverage .
