Cornerstone Community Development Corporation endeavors to participate in the development and revitalization of the community, by addressing the needs of its underrepresented, disenfranchised, & marginalized, through the vehicles of entrepreneurship, partnership, and education.


Entrepreneurship

We are committed to promoting, supporting, and facilitating the genesis, development, and sustained growth of business in the community.


Partnership

We are committed to partnering with various constituents and stakeholders, committed to community wellness, to bring to bear the necessary expertise, resources, and influence to address existing and emerging challenges of the community.


Education

We are committed to promoting, supporting, and facilitating learning as well as access to and dissemination of information throughout the community.
