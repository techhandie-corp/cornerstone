Purpose

The Cornerstone Community Development Corporation is a faith based 501(C)(3) non-profit organization, serving Bergen County New Jersey, whose purpose is to:

■Meet a need(s) in the community
■Physical, natural, and material need that is essential to and promotes wholeness
■Inception and incorporation of the Cornerstone Community Development Corporation (October 2006)
